## 🌟 Hello, Stary World

<br>

Today I'm excited to formally announce what I've been cooking — and what I'll be building for many years to come. 

✨ Introducing **[NULLSTAR](https://github.com/NULLSTAR-GAMES)**, an agentic-native game and intelligence studio — or as I like to call it, the operating system for agentic superintelligence. ✨


What does that mean exactly? I'm building a 7-layer, vertically integrated infrastructure and technology that will fundamentally change how multiplayer games are created and delivered.

This operating system, **PleiadesOS**, introduces advanced procedural content generation for rich, non-linear storytelling — dynamic quests, branching outcomes, and evolving world states governed by flexible rules and alternative physics. Non-player characters (NPCs) are modeled as autonomous agents with persistent memory, goals, and decision-making capabilities. They adapt to player behavior, form social identities, and enable players to co-create their own experience — all built on top of current industry tools and standards.

## 🌟 A Small Glimpse Under the Hood 

<br>

Here is how **PleiadesOS** is designed:

#### 1. MAIA: Graph-based multi-agentic infrastructure SDK, heavily customized on top of Pi, with guardrail, middlewares, and verification loops in Rust - and formal verificated in Lean.
#### 2. ELECTRA: Multi-layered short- and long-term memory module handling internal states, vaults, compaction, and summarization — optimized for fast search and smart retrieval, written in Rust and verified in Lean.
#### 3. TAYGETE: Customizable harness with native support for dozens of tools and several opt-in features, including monetization and prompt caching.
#### 4. ALCYONE: Evals, observability, benchmarks, tests, security hardening, compliance, and dashboards.
#### 5. CELAENO: Novel Rust-based multiplayer deployment through smart coordination of sandboxed subagents.
#### 6. STEROPE: Multimodal module where game engines are integrated.
#### 7. MEROPE: The novel NPC brain.

<br>

My vision is to move gaming beyond static, developer-limited experiences toward scalable, intelligence-driven worlds built for the Age of Agents — and to bring every daring game developer along for the ride.

I'll be dedicating a full post to each module in the future — stay tuned.
